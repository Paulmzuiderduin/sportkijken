Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Sportkijken processes, what analytics are used, and how you can contact the site owner.

Last updated: March 14, 2026 Contact: privacy@paulzuiderduin.com

1. What Sportkijken does

Sportkijken helps visitors find where sports events can be watched in the Netherlands, including TV channels, streaming services, timing, and whether an event is free or paid.

2. Data processed

  • Basic website request information such as IP address, browser, and device metadata needed to deliver the site.
  • Search/filter usage within the website interface.
  • Privacy-friendly website traffic measurement through Umami.
  • Optional Google Analytics 4 data only after consent is granted.

3. Analytics

Sportkijken uses Umami for basic privacy-friendly traffic measurement. This is used to understand overall visits and popular pages.

Sportkijken also uses Google Analytics 4, but only after a visitor gives consent through the on-site analytics consent prompt.

4. Legal basis

Basic site operation and low-impact website measurement are processed on the basis of legitimate interests. Optional Google Analytics 4 is processed only on the basis of consent.

5. Third parties

Sportkijken may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and infrastructure. At the time of writing, this includes:

  • Umami Cloud for privacy-friendly traffic analytics.
  • Google Analytics 4 for optional consent-based analytics.
  • GitHub Pages for hosting and delivery.

6. Your choices

You can reject optional Google Analytics 4 and keep using the site. You can also change your analytics preference later through the privacy controls on the site.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact privacy@paulzuiderduin.com.
